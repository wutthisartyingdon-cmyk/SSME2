FAQ

Helpful starter answers for a public-facing SSME landing page.

What is SSME?

SSME stands for Secret Superpower of Mindful Eating. It is a digital experience designed to help people notice body signals, make calmer food choices, and support more consistent energy throughout the day.

Is this focused on dieting or calorie counting?

No. The tone is meant to be gentle and non-judgmental. The core idea is signals over scores — supporting reflection and better choices rather than rigid tracking.

Who is SSME for?

It is ideal for people who feel energy dips, decision fatigue around food, or want a more mindful and realistic approach to meal planning.
